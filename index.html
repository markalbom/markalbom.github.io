About Me

New Yorker. Coder. Cat Lover

Lifelong New Yorker turned full-stack developer who believes the most important language a developer can learn is the one you use to speak with non-programmers. To me, programming is all about communication from the developer; articulating your goals to the machine, the client, and everyone involved in the life cycle process in between the two. This can always be challenging, but devising creative solutions to difficult problems is what first attracted me to software engineering. As a junior full-stack developer, I work to solve these problems with a clear understanding of technology, clean code and effective communication.
